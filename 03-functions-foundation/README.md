# Functions Foundation: An Expedition to Function-Land

Building on the Foundation Course with Pre-Work, in this book, you will practice and gain knowledge and understanding of working with functions in the JavaScript language. You will apply that knowledge in two small projects and then take a self-assessment to gauge how much you have learned.
